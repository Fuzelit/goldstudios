/06 Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What's included in your packages?

Strategy, design, development, testing, deployment, and 30–90 days of post-launch support. We also provide hosting recommendations and analytics setup at no extra cost.

How long does a project take?

Landing pages: 2–3 weeks. Brand websites: 4–6 weeks. Custom applications: 6–10 weeks. We maintain weekly communication throughout every project.

What does AI integration include?

The Brand Website package includes one AI-powered feature — this could be a chatbot, smart FAQ, content generator, personalization layer, or semantic search, depending on your project needs.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Two rounds of revisions are included in all packages. Additional revisions are available and scoped on a per-round basis.

Will my site be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Every site is built mobile-first and tested across iOS and Android devices at multiple screen sizes.

Can you handle ongoing updates?

Yes. Maintenance packages are available after your support period ends, covering updates, security patches, and performance optimization.
